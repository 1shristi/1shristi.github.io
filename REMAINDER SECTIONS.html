Education


I have a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction from the University of Toronto, where I focused on user-centered design principles and usability testing. My academic background has equipped me with the skills to conduct thorough user research and apply design thinking methodologies effectively.

implementation

Implementation


The design was implemented using Figma, which allowed for rapid prototyping and collaboration with the development team. I created interactive prototypes to demonstrate the user flows and gather feedback from stakeholders. The final design included a series of UI screens that addressed the key pain points identified during the research phase. These screens were designed to be intuitive, user-friendly, and aligned with the client's vision for the new shipping platform.

operations

Operations


The design process involved close collaboration with the development team to ensure that the UI screens were feasible and could be implemented within the existing technical constraints. Regular feedback loops with the PO and key stakeholders helped refine the design and ensure alignment with business goals. The final design was presented to the client, who expressed satisfaction with the user-friendly interface and its potential to improve the efficiency of their shipping operations.

challenges challenges

Challenges


One of the main challenges was the tight deadline of 4 weeks, which required a focused and efficient design process. Additionally, the complexity of the legacy system and the need to translate its functionalities into a modern UI posed significant challenges. Another challenge was ensuring that the new design aligned with the client's vision while also addressing user needs effectively. Balancing these requirements required careful prioritization and iterative feedback throughout the design process.

reflections reflections

Reflections


This project reinforced the importance of user research and iterative design in creating effective UI solutions. The close collaboration with the PO and development team was crucial in ensuring that the design met both user needs and technical feasibility. I learned that even with a tight deadline, a focused and structured design process can yield valuable results. The positive feedback from the client and stakeholders validated the effectiveness of the design approach and its potential impact on improving shipping operations.
